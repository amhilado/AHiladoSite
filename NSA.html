NSA Website Redesign

Goal: Redesign the look and layout of a government agency website.

Project Focus: User Interface Design

Software Used: Sketch

Final Deliverables: UI Redesign Prototypes

Design Thinking

For this project the objective was to redesign the NSA website, as the current website needed a little update. The NSA is responsible for global monitoring, collection, and processing of information and data for foreign and domestic intelligence and counterintelligence purposes.

My goal for this project was to update the design in order to align with the values of the NSA and what they represent. With that in mind, I wanted the website and style guide to give a sense of professionalism, security, honesty, and clarity.

I decided to go for a simple yet refined color palette of blues, whites, and an orange as a contrast color in order to give the site that sense of professionalism and security.

Color Palette

From a user's perspective, the current website was rather overwhelming and cluttered, and a little hard to navigate. To help make this process of finding more information about the NSA, I decided to display the information in a clear and easy to comprehend manner. I simplified the page layout, used a typeface that was easy to read, included larger images, and gave some of the content a little more breathing room.

Future Considerations

Going forward with this project, I would like to conduct user tests with the new layout, design, and color palette, and determine whether or not the process of finding information on the website has improved, and what can also be improved upon.
